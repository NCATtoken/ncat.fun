FEATURES

Community Built

Community driven & fair launch. Dev burned all team tokens and plans to introduce a DAO to propose/vote on direction of project!

LP Grinder

5% of every transaction contributes toward automatically generating further liquidity for the NCAT Community. Benefiting long term holders the most!

Frictionless Yield

4% of every transaction is taken and re distributed to all NCAT holders. The burn address is also a holder thus each transaction helps deflate the supply.

Highly Secured

All initial liquidity provided has been locked with UniCrypt. The contract owner has been renounced, leaving the contract trustless for the NCAT community.
